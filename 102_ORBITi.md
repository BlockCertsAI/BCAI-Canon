# ORBITi — The Browser for the Authenticated Internet

## What Is ORBITi

ORBITi is the **native browser and routing layer** of BlockCertsAI.

It is the access point to the authenticated internet — where identity, data, and computation are verified at the foundation.

While Web3 applications still depend on Web2 browsers like Chrome or Safari, ORBITi removes the **final point of centralization**.

Every interaction — from discovery to execution — occurs inside a decentralized, identity-authenticated environment.

---

## Why Browsers Were the Last Choke Point

Even “decentralized” systems fail if access runs through centralized browsers.

Web2 browsers enable:
- anonymous sessions
- credential harvesting
- cookie-based surveillance
- behavioral profiling
- ad-driven manipulation
- bot traffic and fake engagement

ORBITi replaces the browser itself.

## Core Capabilities

Every ORBITi session begins with **Proof of Authentication (PoA)**.

- no passwords  
- no forms  
- no anonymous sessions  

Identity is verified at the blockchain layer before interaction begins.

---

### Zero Tracking, Zero Surveillance
ORBITi has:
- no cookies  
- no tracking scripts  
- no profiling  
- no data extraction  

Privacy is not a setting.
It is structural.

ORBITi provides direct access to:

- DomainCERTin addresses

- private Secure Virtual Spaces

All access is authenticated by design.

### User-Owned Data Flow
All activity routes through the user’s **Secure Virtual Space (SVS)**.

- no centralized servers  
- no platform-owned sessions  
- no exposed data paths  

The user controls the flow — not the browser, not the site, not the platform.

- no ads  
- no subscriptions

Utility is paid for transparently, based on actual use.

- no bots

Discovery becomes accountable, auditable, and economically aligned.
